Koliery - Колеры in belarussian - meaning colors is a game inspired by the
Sega Columns and the ISS Colours for the Atari 500.

It was developed using pixi.js in 2020 while I was in Minsk Belarus,
but not released until 2024.

It was created using pixi.js and a lot of open and free assets!

                This is a free game licensed under GPLv3


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To play online just visit: https://mateus.digital/koliery
    or run a local webserver in the downloaded folder.
